# Our Family Recipe Calculator
### Created June 2026

A simple recipe list with a timing calculator, for our regular meals. This replaces the pieces of card and paper in our kitchen. 

The problem it solves is by having a quick calculation for the timings based on the different sizes of meal, which is the main thing that changes for our roast meals.


## AI Assisted
This project was created by making a specification, having JetBrains AI validate it, and suggest improvements, and then using the AI tools step by step to generate the project.

This was to improve my skills and knowledge of AI-accelerated development.

I could have hand-coded this in a couple of days. Using AI this took about 5 hours of time, including making adjustments and improvements to the first prototype page.

I made a number of small improvements along the way, each time using AI assistance.

See my original [spec.md](spec.md) I used to develop the starting specification to work from.

As I usually do, I kept a short [diary.md](diary.md) of my steps and progress - something I really recommend doing when developing your own small projects!
